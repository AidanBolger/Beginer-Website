Examples of technologies:

The Star Wars franchise has been known to inspire and influence real-life technology development. Here are some examples of Star Wars technology that have been mirrored or inspired by real-life technology.

Robotics:

The droids in Star Wars, such as R2-D2 and C-3PO, have inspired real-life robotics. Companies like Boston Dynamics are developing advanced robots for a variety of applications, including manufacturing, healthcare, and search and rescue.

Holograms:

The Star Wars universe features characters communicating with each other through holographic images. In real life, we have seen advances in holographic technology, which can be used in teleconferencing, military purposes and entertainment.

Prosthetics:

The character of Luke Skywalker receives a prosthetic hand in Star Wars Episode V: The Empire Strikes Back. Today, we have advanced prosthetic technology that can replace missing limbs and provide better mobility and dexterity to amputees.

Lasers:

In Star Wars, blaster guns and lightsabers use laser technology. While we don't have real-life lightsabers, laser technology is used in medicine, communications, and military applications. A comon example being eye surgery.
